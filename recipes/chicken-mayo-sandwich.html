Chicken mayo sandwich

A creamy and balanced chicken mayo sandwich

The base of this recipe is strong with juicy, flavorful chicken breast mixed with a luscious sauce thatâ€™s creamy, savory, and tangy in just the right balance

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • shredded chicken breast
  • salt and black pepper
  • mayo
  • bell peppers
  • bread

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken with mayonnaise
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread a layer of mayonnaise on one slice of bread
  4. Add the chicken filling, followed by bell peppers
  5. Cover with the other slice of bread
  6. Enjoy
