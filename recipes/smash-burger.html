Smash burger

The utlimate smashed ground beef burger

They’re minced beef and salt, smashed flat over a searingly hot flat-grill until you get a crispy, craggy-edged, asymmetric, cooked-through patty that’s juicy and caramelised

Ingredients

  • 1 soft hamburger roll, buttered and toasted
  • oppings as desired, such as mayonnaise, mustard, shredded lettuce, onions, tomatoes, and pickles
  • 110 g freshly ground beef chuck, divided into 2 balls
  • 1 slice good melting cheese,

Steps

  1. Prepare burger bun by laying toppings on bottom half of bun.
  2. Preheat a large stainless steel sauté pan or skillet over high heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Place balls of beef in pan and smash down with a stiff metal spatula, using a second spatula to add pressure. Smashed patties should be slightly wider than burger bun.
  4. Season generously with salt and pepper and allow to cook until patties are well browned and tops are beginning to turn pale pink/gray in spots, about 45 seconds
  5. Flip patties and immediately place a slice of cheese over 1 patty, then stack the second directly on top
  6. Immediately remove from pan and transfer to waiting burger bun. Serve
