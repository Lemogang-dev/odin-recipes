Pap-and-Wors

Pap with a healthy amount of gravy drizzles on top

Pap and wors is a traditional South African dish, featuring a hearty maize porridge (pap) alongside a flavorful sausage, typically boerewors

Ingredients

  • Maize meal
  • 3-4 cups water
  • 1 onicn
  • tomato paste or 2 tomatoes
  • spices and salt
  • boerewors

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot, once boiling add salt
  2. Add maize meal the the water and stir until you like texture
  3. For the wors, pour small amount of water in a pan and add the wors
  4. The water will turn into oil so the wors can fry
  5. Once fried, remove wors from pan then add chopped onions for the gravy
  6. ry for 4 min then add tomato paste and stir
  7. Add little of water and soup for the gravy to thicken then serve
